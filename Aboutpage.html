HANGMAN 2 an Ultimate Challenge is a 2Player network game.
A Different Game From Traditional one .Here both Players play simultaneously.
Rules:
1)You have to search for a player.
2)Then Provide a word For Your Opponent.
3) Then Guess a word to win.

New Features:
1)The one How completes the Hangman will be decleared as a Loser and the opponent will be decleared as a Winner automatically.
2)The one How guesses the right word first will be decleared as a Winner and the opponent will be decleared as a Loser automatically.
